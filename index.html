📖 How to Play
  1. Enter your name below so your results can be saved.
  2. Choose a timer (1–5 min) and how many words per round.
  3. Each round gives you random nouns and a grammar point to practice.
  4. Write a sentence using all the words and the grammar structure shown.
  5. Hit Submit before time runs out — or it auto-submits for you!
  6. At the end, export or print your results to share with your teacher.
